About

How this site works: you say what you like; your friends say what they like; the site tells you what you have in common.

Why? Say I have a terrible secret that I like singing along to S-Club 7, which I will never admit to in public. If any of my friends are also into that, then we can find each other and have secret S-Club karaoke sessions. My other friends who don't like S-Club will never know :D

This isn't foolproof; somebody could claim to like everything just to see what matches they get - but if one of your friends does that, you should punch them in the face until they stop doing that :3
